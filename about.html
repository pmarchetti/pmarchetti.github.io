Philip is a product manager, designer, and aspiring bagel baker from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He draws on a breadth of knowledge and experience spanning product management, innovation strategy, design thinking, and architecture. He is a graduate of Carnegie Mellon University with a master's degree in product development (integrated innovation) and the University of Kentucky with a bachelor's degree in architecture.

He is based in Pittsburgh and currently works with Uber ATG, where he is a product manager working on building Uber's self-driving vehicle offering. Check us out here. Previously, he spent 5+ years in the NYC-area working in product, innovation, & strategy at two Fortune 500 companies, teaching and mentoring at universities in the region, and a stint in innovation consulting with a startups
