Philip is a designer, product specialist, and strategist from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He draws on a breadth of knowledge spanning product management, strategy, design thinking, business, and architecture. He is a graduate of Carnegie Mellon University with a master's degree in product development (integrated innovation) and the University of Kentucky with a bachelor's degree in architecture.

He is based in New York and currently works with Verizon Open Innovation, where he manages programs that bring innovative thinking into the company through new product development and recruiting.
