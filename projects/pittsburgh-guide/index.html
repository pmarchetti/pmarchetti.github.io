About The Project


How do you let 200+ people know where to go when you invite them to your hometown for your wedding? You can field requests one-by-one or you can be proactive and build a Pittsburgh Guide that shows off your favorite neighborhoods, spots, and things to do. A fun night & weekend project turned into a useful artifact for friends and family—and even led to a few friends trying to "check off the whole list."
